What is the definition of pre-workout?

Pre-workout pills, sometimes known as "pre-workouts," are meant to provide you with a boost of energy before you exercise. The bulk of them includes caffeine as a key ingredient, and these supplements are often in tablet or powder form. Pre-workout pills contain substances that may be beneficial to your health and workouts. However, the supplements may have adverse side effects, which you should be aware of.
